ABOUT US

The Design and Innovation Club (DI) is a dynamic collective of computer science undergraduates dedicated to fostering curiosity and expertise in innovative design thinking. Established in 2019, we've hosted numerous workshops and events, covering subjects such as Photoshop, poster design, animation, and more. Our primary objectives include equipping individuals with contemporary design thinking for real-world challenges and fostering the learning and teaching of innovative design paired with technical solutions. Join us on this creative journey!
