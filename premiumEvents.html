HACKFEST 2.0

HACKFEST




Hackfest 2.0, held in the academic year 2023-2024, was a dynamic two-day Hackathon contest for college students, organized by dedicated faculty members Mrs. M. Indumathy and Mrs. Punitha D. It featured two rounds - an online abstract submission and an in-campus hackathon. The event's distinguished chief guest was Dr. M. Jayaprakasan, CEO of Naan Mudhalvan Skill Initiative, and the hackathon mentor was Mr. Bharathi Athinarayanan from IQVIA. With a theme focused on innovation and problem-solving, Hackfest 2.0 drew a total of 131 participants, with exciting rounds of competition leading to the announcement of the winners during a grand valedictory ceremony.


Wondering what Hackgrid is?

Apple Developers Group's flagship event App-a-thon has been rebranded as Hackgrid. It's a 36 hours long hack-a-thon where participants can develop applications on various platforms (including web apps). We, at Hackgrid encourage use of technologies like AI, Blockchain, ML, IoT, Algorithms, AR/VR and Image Processing.
